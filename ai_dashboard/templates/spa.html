🤖 About AI Dashboard · 关于
Built by Jason Antonio · 由 Jason Antonio 开发
Why was this built? · 为什么要建立这个平台？
This platform was created to bridge the gap between advanced AI technology and Chinese business operations. Many businesses in China and Southeast Asia need intelligent tools but lack accessible, user-friendly solutions.

这个平台的创建旨在弥合先进人工智能技术与中国商业运营之间的差距。中国及东南亚的许多企业需要智能工具，但缺乏易于访问、用户友好的解决方案。
🎯 Objective · 目标
To empower businesses with 9 powerful AI tools that analyze Chinese customer reviews, predict sales, detect fake reviews, recommend products, and more — all in one beautiful, bilingual platform.

为企业提供 9个强大的AI工具，用于分析中文客户评论、预测销售、检测虚假评论、推荐产品等——全部集成在一个美观的双语平台上。
🛠️ Tools Included · 包含工具
Sentiment Analysis · 情感分析  ·  Keyword Extractor · 关键词提取  ·  OCR Scanner · 文字识别  ·  Sales Forecast · 销售预测  ·  Churn Prediction · 流失预测  ·  Recommendations · 商品推荐  ·  Fake Review Detector · 虚假评论检测  ·  Image Classifier · 图像分类  ·  Price Prediction · 价格预测
